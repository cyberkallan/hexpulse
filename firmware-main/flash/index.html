Connect your ESP device via USB-C to deploy the HexPulse system directly from your browser. No external tools required.

📖 Installation Guide

  • Plug it in: Connect your ESP device to your computer using a high-quality USB-C data cable.
  • Deploy: Click the "DEPLOY HEXPULSE" button above.
  • Select Port: A browser popup will appear. Select the COM port that corresponds to your device.
  • Wait: Do not unplug your device while the progress bar is active. It takes about 2 minutes.
  • Boot: Once complete, your device will automatically reboot into HexPulse.

🔧 Troubleshooting

  • No COM Port? Make sure you are using a USB data cable, not just a charging cable. You may also need CH340 or CP2102 drivers depending on your board.
  • Connection Error? Some ESP devices require you to hold the "BOOT" button while connecting the USB cable to enter flash mode.
  • Browser Support: This tool utilizes the Web Serial API. It is strictly supported on Chrome, Edge, and Opera. It will not work on Safari or Firefox.
